About HORODA GmbH

HORODA is a technology-oriented company with extensive experience in scientific-technical development and consulting. As a patent holder for energy-efficient cooling systems for High Performance Computing (HPC), artificial intelligence and aerospace communications, HORODA unites deep know-how and technical expertise.

Since 2025 HORODA operates the HORODA Vacuum division focusing on vacuum and thin film technologies. In close partnership with INFICON AG we distribute and consult on advanced measurement and sensor solutions for demanding research and production applications.
