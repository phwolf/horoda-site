About HORODA GmbH

Founded in 2012 as HORODA UG and restructured as a GmbH in 2025, the company now focuses on vacuum technology, thin film applications, and vacuum coating systems - particularly in the semiconductor, optics, display, and industrial coatingsectors. In addition to its core business, HORODA provides strategic consulting in the fields of machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks, especially at the interface between science and industry. The company originally emerged from the development of energy-efficient cooling technologies for high-performance computing (HPC), including patented systems used in large-scale projects like the LOEWE-CSC (Goethe University Frankfurt) and the Green IT Cube (GSI Helmholtz Centre).

HORODA has also contributed to technology innovation boards in the automotive and aerospace sectors and remains active in the innovation advisory board of peiker Holding GmbH.

Today, HORODA offers a focused portfolio of vacuum and thin film measurement systems and components, serving applications in:

  • Semiconductor and Photonics
  • Display and OLED production
  • Life Sciences and Medical Technology
  • Optical and Industrial Coating
  • Research & Development
  • Aerospace, Automotive, and Materials Science

With deep technical expertise and close ties to both research institutions and industrial partners, HORODA is your reliable partner for high-end process technology.
