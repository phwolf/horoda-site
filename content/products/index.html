Products & Solutions

HORODA GmbH offers high-quality instrumentation and components for demanding vacuum and thin film applications. Our portfolio is focused on three key areas

Thin Film Technology

For use in display, optics, semiconductor, solar, and industrial coating processes

Vacuum Measurement & Control

For accurate monitoring and control of vacuum processes

Vacuum Components

High-performance vacuum components - standardized and reliable

Thin Film Technology

  • Controllers
  • For precise control of film thickness and deposition rate - ensuring stable and repeatable processes.
  • Monitors
  • Real-time monitoring during deposition - essential for consistent process quality.
  • Sensors & Feedthroughs
  • Robust single and multi-sensor solutions for accurate measurements under vacuum conditions.
  • Quartz Monitor Crystals
  • Consumable crystals for reliable measurement of film thickness and deposition rates.

Vacuum Measurement & Control

  • Capacitance Manometers
  • Precise, pressure-independent vacuum measurement in high and medium vacuum ranges.
  • Pirani Gauges
  • Thermal sensors for rough to medium vacuum - versatile and reliable.
  • Cold Cathode Gauges
  • Rugged sensors for high to ultra-high vacuum - low maintenance and long service life.
  • Hot Ionization Gauges
  • High-precision measurement in ultra-high vacuum - ideal for demanding research and industrial use.
  • Vacuum Switches
  • Devices for switching at defined pressure levels - enabling automated process control.
  • Vacuum Gauge Controllers
  • Universal display and control units compatible with various vacuum sensors - with flexible interface options.

Vacuum Components

  • Feedthroughs
  • Durable and sealed feedthroughs for use in demanding vacuum environments.
  • ISO-KF
  • Flexible, quick-clamp connections for small to mid-sized vacuum systems.
  • ISO-K & ISO-F
  • Sturdy components for industrial systems requiring mechanical stability and secure connections.
  • ISO-CF
  • Designed for ultra-high vacuum (UHV) applications - offering excellent sealing and corrosion resistance.
